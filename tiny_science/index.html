TINY SCIENCE

» Teamwork @ EdLab

» Role: Visual designer & Theater Sound/Light Production & Concept Development

» Collaboration: Hollingworth Science Camp, Smith Laerning Theater Production Team

» 2019 Summer

Campers in grades one through five at the Hollingworth Science Camp shrink down to learn about big ideas surrounding nanotechnology in this immersive theater experience.

As a part of the production team, I developed the theater experience based on the scripts from Hollingworth Science Camp with the group, delievered visual design for the environment, created projection mapping, and assisted with light and sound control during the camp.
